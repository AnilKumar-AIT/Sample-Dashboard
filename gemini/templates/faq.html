Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the FallVision ecosystem.

How accurate is the limb angle detection? ▼
FallVision utilizes high-frequency IoT sensor data processed through our proprietary neural-link algorithm. Our current baseline accuracy for limb propulsion angles is within 1.2 degrees of clinical-grade lab equipment.
Can my caregiver see my data? ▼
Yes, through the "Emergency Hub," you can authorize specific contacts. They will receive automated alerts if the system detects a significant "Stability Drift" or a potential fall event.
Is my neuro-data secure? ▼
We use AES-256 encryption for all data transmission and PBKDF2 hashing for authentication. Your gait signature is anonymized before clinical analysis.
